Philosophy

We want to improve as Front-End Developers and we know that we need to practice many hours to achieve it. Unfortunately our daily schedules donÂ´t let us enough free time left to study as much as we'd like, so we have come up with a solution: the frontGirlsSessions.

We believe in learning by doing. Building front-end skills requires practice and pushing outside our comfort zone. We all agree that learning should be fun.

Methodology

We are going to schedule frontGirlsSessions in our friday afternoons. In this sessions, we are going to study and practice not only Sass and PostCSS, but git, grunt/gulp, npm and all the new technologies we can potentially need for our daily work.

The responsability of preparing the sessions will be of one of the frontGirls each week. It's simple: each of us studies once a month but gets 4 lessons.
